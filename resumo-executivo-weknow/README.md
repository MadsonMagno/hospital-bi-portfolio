# Case Study: Hospital Executive Summary

> A consolidated executive dashboard providing the Board of Directors with a single source of truth for hospital financial, operational, and clinical performance.

## The Challenge
Hospital executives often must review multiple, fragmented reports across billing, nursing, inventory, and human resources to understand the overarching status of the organization. This fragmentation delays critical strategic decisions and obscures the correlation between different areas, such as how an increase in ICU occupancy might affect overall medication costs.

## The Solution
I built the "Executive Pulse" dashboard, which serves as the hospital's central management cockpit.
* **Tech Stack:** Weknow BI.
* **Key Feature:** High-level KPI aggregation pulling top-level metrics from specialized sub-dashboards (such as Average Ticket, Profitability, and Occupancy).
* **Methodology:** Implemented visual indicators and trend analysis to track month-over-month performance against strategic goals.

## Results & Impact
* **Executive Visibility:** Provided a proxy EBITDA calculation based on the aggregated net result of all patient accounts.
* **Operational Insight:** Summarized overall hospital efficiency through global bed turnover and patient throughput metrics.
* **Risk Management:** Established real-time alert systems for critical operational risks, such as imminent shortages of life-saving medications.
