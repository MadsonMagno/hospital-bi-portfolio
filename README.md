# Healthcare Business Intelligence Portfolio

This repository contains a collection of 8 Business Intelligence case studies focused on hospital operations, clinical flow, and financial governance. These projects demonstrate the application of data engineering and analytics to solve complex challenges within large-scale healthcare environments, specifically using the MV ERP and Weknow BI platforms.

## Portfolio Overview

Each project is organized into its own directory with detailed documentation, sanitized SQL logic, and synthetic datasets for demonstration.

### Financial & Revenue Governance
* **[Average Ticket Management](./ticket-medio):** Optimization of revenue per patient encounter and billing account.
* **[Hospital Profitability](./rentabilidade-hospitalar):** Integration of operational costs with billing data for net-profit tracking.
* **[Pending Account Balances](./saldo-contas-pendentes):** Reduction of "Revenue in Transit" through account aging analysis.
* **[Patient Billing Reconciliation](./gerencial-computo-pacientes):** Governance system to prevent revenue leakage from clinical procedures.

### Operational & Clinical Excellence
* **[Hospital Occupancy & LOS](./ocupacao-hospitalar):** Real-time monitoring of bed availability and Length of Stay (LOS).
* **[Emergency Wait Times](./tempo-espera-atendimento):** Process-mining to identify bottlenecks in triage and registration.
* **[Inventory Valuation](./valores-estoque):** Financial management of medical supplies and inventory rotation.

### Executive Strategy
* **[Executive Summary Dashboard](./resumo-executivo-weknow):** A consolidated view of hospital performance for board-level decision-making.

## Technical Environment
* **Platform:** MV ERP (Oracle-based)
* **BI Tool:** Weknow BI / Power BI
* **Core Languages:** Oracle SQL, Python (Data Simulation)

## Data Integrity & Privacy
To maintain the confidentiality and sensitivity of hospital and patient data, all clinical information in this repository has been replaced with **synthetic (mock) data**. The SQL queries have been sanitized to showcase logic and architectural expertise without exposing proprietary database structures or credentials.
